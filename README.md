# Soft Skills Resources for Tech Professionals

A comprehensive 30-60 day actionable guide for developing critical soft skills, specifically designed for experienced tech professionals (20+ years experience).

## Overview

This repository contains a structured soft skills development program with:
- **10 key soft skills** identified as critical for senior tech professionals
- **3-5 real-world activities** per skill for hands-on practice
- **Reflection prompts** to encourage deeper learning and self-awareness
- **Curated resources** including books, videos/TED Talks, podcasts, and online courses
- Resources are current and accessible (mix of 2021-2024 updated content and timeless classics)

## The Guide

The main guide is available as a CSV file: [`soft-skills-guide-30-60-days.csv`](soft-skills-guide-30-60-days.csv)

### 10 Key Soft Skills Covered

1. **Strategic Communication** - Effectively conveying complex technical concepts to diverse audiences
2. **Emotional Intelligence** - Understanding and managing emotions in yourself and others
3. **Adaptive Leadership** - Leading through change and uncertainty
4. **Mentorship and Coaching** - Developing others and building organizational capability
5. **Conflict Resolution** - Addressing disagreements constructively
6. **Strategic Thinking** - Seeing the big picture and aligning with organizational goals
7. **Inclusive Collaboration** - Building and leading diverse teams effectively
8. **Resilience and Stress Management** - Maintaining effectiveness under pressure
9. **Negotiation and Influence** - Achieving objectives through persuasion and consensus
10. **Continuous Learning and Adaptability** - Staying current and embracing new challenges

## How to Use This Guide

### 30-Day Intensive Track
- Focus on **5 skills** (weeks 1-4)
- Complete 2-3 activities per skill
- Read/watch 1-2 resources per skill
- Weekly reflection sessions

### 60-Day Comprehensive Track
- Cover **all 10 skills** (weeks 1-8)
- Complete 3-4 activities per skill
- Engage with 2-3 resources per skill
- Bi-weekly reflection and adjustment

### Self-Paced Track
- Choose skills based on current needs
- Complete activities in any order
- Mix and match resources
- Regular reflection (monthly recommended)

## CSV Structure

The CSV file contains the following columns:
- **Skill** - Name of the soft skill
- **Description** - Brief explanation of the skill and its importance
- **Activities** - 3-5 real-world, actionable activities (numbered list)
- **Reflection** - Thought-provoking question to deepen learning
- **Book** - Current, highly-rated book recommendation
- **Video** - TED Talk or educational video
- **Podcast** - Relevant podcast for ongoing learning
- **Course** - Online course from platforms like Coursera, LinkedIn Learning, Udemy, edX

## Tips for Success

1. **Start with assessment** - Identify which skills need the most development
2. **Schedule activities** - Block time in your calendar for practice
3. **Find accountability** - Partner with a peer or mentor
4. **Document progress** - Keep a journal of insights and improvements
5. **Apply immediately** - Look for opportunities to practice in your daily work
6. **Iterate** - Come back to skills periodically as you grow

## Resource Selection Criteria

All resources included meet the following criteria:
- ✅ Published or updated 2021-2024 (books, courses) OR recognized as timeless classics (some TED Talks)
- ✅ Highly rated (4.5+ stars on major platforms)
- ✅ Accessible (available on major platforms)
- ✅ Relevant to experienced professionals
- ✅ Actionable and practical

Note: While we prioritize recent resources, some classic TED Talks and foundational content are included due to their enduring relevance and impact in the field.
